The analyses in this app rely on appropriately named columns. Your original columns may have been renamed internally. Check that the final dataframe shown contains at least the following columns. If any of these are missing, please contact the developers, as the following pages will not function properly.

  • contact_id: A unique ID for each case

  • linked_case_id: A unique ID for each contact

  • date_of_last_contact: The date of last contact between the case and the contact

  • admin_1: Admin level 1 of the contact

  • admin_2: Admin level 2 of the contact

  • follow_up_date:Dates of follow-up for monitored contacts

  • follow_up_status: Follow-up status for each day that contacts are monitored
